# Brr Privacy Policy

[🇹🇷 Türkçe sürüm](./privacy)

**Last updated: July 3, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how your personal data is collected, used,
and protected when you use the Brr mobile application ("App").

**Data Controller:** Burak Yıldırım
**Contact:** burak23369@gmail.com

## 1. Data We Collect

### When creating an account:
- Email address and password (your password is stored securely and cannot
  be viewed by us)

### When creating your profile:
- Name (display name)
- Date of birth (for age calculation and 18+ verification)
- Gender and match preference
- Profile photos
- About (bio) text

### While using the App:
- **Location:** Your approximate location (rounded to ~1 km precision) is
  used to show people near you. Your exact location is never stored or
  shown to other users; only the approximate distance between you is
  displayed.
- **Messages:** Your conversations with matched users are stored on our
  servers to provide the service.
- **Interactions:** In-app decisions such as like, pass, and freeze
  (Freezer).
- **Block and report records:** Kept for safety purposes.

### Automatically collected data:
- **Purchase status:** Your premium subscription status is processed via
  our payment infrastructure, RevenueCat. Your credit card details never
  reach us; payments are processed by the Apple App Store.
- **Crash and error data:** Technical crash reports (device model, OS
  version, error details) are collected via Sentry to detect app errors.
  Personal identifiers such as IP addresses are not included.
- **Push notification token:** A device-specific notification address
  (push token) is stored to deliver notifications.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- To create and manage your account
- To show you suitable profiles near you
- To provide matching and messaging features
- To deliver notifications for new likes, matches, and messages
- To manage your premium subscription
- To ensure safety and detect violations
- To detect and fix app errors

We **do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising
purposes.**

## 3. Service Providers We Share Data With

The App uses the following infrastructure providers to operate:

| Provider | Purpose | Data location |
|---|---|---|
| Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Functions) | Authentication, database, photo storage | Frankfurt, Germany (europe-west3)* |
| RevenueCat | Subscription management | USA |
| Sentry | Crash reporting | Germany (EU region) |
| Apple App Store | Payment processing | Apple infrastructure |
| Expo Push Service | Push notification delivery | USA |

*Account deletion is triggered via a function in the us-central1 (USA)
region due to technical requirements; your data remains stored in
Frankfurt.

These providers process your data solely to provide services to us.

## 4. Information Visible to Other Users

- Your name, age (calculated from date of birth), photos, and bio
- The approximate distance between you (e.g., "5 km away")
- Your full date of birth, email, and exact location are never shown to
  any user.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

- Your data is retained as long as your account is active.
- **When you delete your account** (Profile → Delete Account), your
  profile, photos, messages, likes, matches, and records in other users'
  lists are automatically deleted.
- Deleted data cannot be recovered, except where legally required.

## 6. Data Security

Your data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Database access is
protected by security rules that only permit authorized operations. Your
password is stored as an irreversible hash.

## 7. Age Requirement

Brr is only for users **18 years of age or older**. Accounts found to
belong to users under 18 are closed and their data deleted.
